    What is Patch & Stitch

    Tucked away on a quiet street in the heart of Daveyton township on the east of Johannesburg, you’ll find Patch and Stitch.

    We have been offering community services that help extend clothing’s lifespan, save money and reduce clothing waste, everything from garment dyeing to repairs and alterations.

    • Garment Alteration
    • Garment Repairing
    • Garment Dyeing & Up-cycling

    Patch And Stitch solves problems. We recognised that a lot of people cannot afford to buy clothes on a regular basis. It’s much more affordable to get clothes repaired and refurbished. And, some people have clothes that they consider to be old or out of fashion, so we up-cycle them. Others have difficulties finding their perfect size at retail outlets, so they bring their purchases to us for alterations.

    Patch & Stitch Workshops

    About Patch & Stitch

    Patch and Stitch is a grassroots organisation committed to raising awareness about the environmental and social impact of the fashion industry. We aim to inspire individuals to adopt sustainable fashion practices and make conscious choices that align with their values. Our workshops provide practical knowledge, resources, and skills to empower participants to embrace sustainable fashion and contribute to a more ethical and environmentally friendly future.

    Workshop Overview

    "A Sustainable Fashion Journey" is an immersive series of workshops designed to educate participants about the significance of sustainable and ethical fashion while empowering them to make informed choices. The workshops delve into the lifecycle of garments, exploring sustainable production practices, responsible sourcing, and conscious consumption. Through interactive sessions, hands-on activities, and engaging discussions, participants will learn how to extend the lifespan of clothes, up-cycle old garments into new creations, and build a sustainable and ethical wardrobe.

    Objectives

    • Raise Awareness: To increase understanding of the environmental impact of the fashion industry and the importance of sustainable practices.
    • Promote Sustainable and ethical fashion: To educate participants about responsible sourcing, production, and consumption of clothes.
    • Encourage Collaboration: To foster a partnership between Patch and Stitch and collaborators in promoting sustainable fashion.
    • Provide Practical Skills: To equip participants with the knowledge and techniques to extend the lifespan of clothes through repairs, alterations, dyeing and up-cycling.
